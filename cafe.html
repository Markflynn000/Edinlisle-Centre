Welcome to the Edinlisle Cafe!


Our farm café is the perfect place to slow down and enjoy the produce we’re so proud of. Using ingredients straight from the shop, we serve a selection of simple, boutique-style meals made fresh each day.


From hearty breakfasts with our free-range eggs and locally reared bacon, to light lunches with homemade breads, cheeses and seasonal vegetables, everything is prepared with care and flavour in mind.


You’ll also find freshly baked cakes, pastries and barista-style coffee, alongside treats like handmade chocolates, local jams and honey.


Whether you’re stopping in after a walk or taking a break from exploring, it’s a cosy spot to sit back and enjoy the best of the farm.
