A whole host of Activities to try!


There's a whole host of activities to take part in at The Edinlisle Centre.


How will you choose to unwind?



Petting Zoo

Get up close with our friendly farm animals. Meet goats, sheep and more, and see how they’re looked after day to day. It’s a relaxed, hands-on experience that’s always a favourite with visitors. Take your time getting to know each animal and enjoy those small, memorable moments.

Playpark

The outdoor playpark is a great place for kids to burn off some energy. There’s plenty of space to run around, climb and explore, while parents can take a moment to sit back and watch. It’s an easy, fun stop between activities and a place children naturally gravitate back to throughout the day.

Walks & Trails

Explore the farm through a network of peaceful walks and trails. Wander through open fields, spot local wildlife, and take in the changing scenery as you go. Whether it’s a gentle stroll or a longer wander, it’s a chance to slow down and enjoy a bit of nature.
