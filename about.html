Welcome to Edinlisle!


Welcome to the Edinlisle Centre, a converted farm with a rich history and a mission to give back to the local community. Established in 1901, the farm originally provided essential commodities to the area, and today it continues to serve the community in new ways. Our centre hosts a café, a shop, and a whole host of activities for you to enjoy. Everyone is welcome, our aim is to make this a place for all.


In our farm shop, you’ll find a wide variety of fresh, locally produced goods. From free-range eggs laid by our own chickens to pork reared in the fields around the house, every product has a distinct, authentic flavour. Choose from award-winning pies with fillings such as apricot or blackberry, hand-made chocolates, local honey and marmalade, homemade breads and cakes, and locally sourced fruit juices, wines, beers, and ciders. We also offer fresh vegetables, lovingly grown in our garden.


Come and explore the Edinlisle Centre. Where history, community, and fresh local produce come together.
